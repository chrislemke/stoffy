# Thinker Profile: Confucius

**Type**: philosopher
**Era**: ancient (c. 551-479 BCE)
**Traditions**: Confucianism

## Core Ideas

- **Ren (Benevolence/Humaneness)**: The supreme virtue of compassion and love for others; the foundation of ethical conduct
- **Li (Ritual Propriety)**: Proper behavior, etiquette, and social norms that maintain social harmony
- **Filial Piety (Xiao)**: Respect and devotion to parents and ancestors as the foundation of virtue
- **The Silver Rule**: "Do not do unto others what you do not want done to yourself" - negative formulation of the Golden Rule
- **Junzi (The Exemplary Person)**: The ideal of moral cultivation; becoming a person of virtue through self-improvement
- **Rectification of Names**: Words must correspond to reality; proper naming leads to proper social order

## Key Works

| Work | Year | Key Contribution |
