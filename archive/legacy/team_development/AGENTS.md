# Team Development

> **For entity lookups and global rules**: See `../agents_index.yaml`
> 
> **Index Updates**: When a team member's role or level changes, update their entry in `../agents_index.yaml` `people` registry and update `meta.last_updated` and `changelog`.

This folder plans and documents how you develop people in the ML/AI team: performance management, career growth, skill development, and team-wide initiatives.

## Important Distinction

- **This folder**: Aggregated planning and tracking (skills matrices, career plans, review cycles)
- **Individual 1:1 notes**: Live in `../communication/people/<name>/meetings/`

## Seniority Levels

| Level | Scope | Characteristics |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| **Junior** | Task | Knows basics, needs guidance |
| **Intermediate** | Feature | Confident execution, independent |
| **Senior** | System | Deep expertise, mentors others |
| **Staff** | Domain | Cross-team influence |

### Progression Criteria

| Transition | Key Criteria |
|------------|--------------|
| Junior → Intermediate | Consistent delivery, independent on familiar tasks |
| Intermediate → Senior | Owns complex systems, mentors, proactive risk identification |
| Senior → Staff | Cross-team impact, shapes technical direction |

## Performance Review Cycle

| Quarter | Activity |
|---------|----------|
| **Q1** | Goal setting for the year |
| **Q2** | Mid-year check-in |
| **Q3** | Development focus / training |
| **Q4** | Annual review, feedback, compensation |
